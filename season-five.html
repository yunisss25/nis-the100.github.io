In Season 5 of The 100, six years after a nuclear meltdown, a prison ship arrives on the last survivable land on Earth. Clarke, living with a young Nightblood named Madi, must contend with the returning survivors from space and the bunker. A struggle erupts between the prisoners and the newly united Wonkru clan over control of the valley, ultimately leading to its destruction. The survivors escape to space, enter cryosleep, and head to a new planet after realizing Earth won't recover​​
