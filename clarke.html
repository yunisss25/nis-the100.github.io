Clarke Griffin

Leader of the Sky People

Earth, Post-Apocalypse

Clarke Griffin is a natural leader and one of the original 100 delinquents sent to Earth from the Ark. Known for her bravery and resourcefulness, Clarke has played a pivotal role in the survival of her people, navigating complex relationships and moral dilemmas in a harsh new world.
